Location

Thanks for stopping by. We are the last authentic Unique restaurant in Nsukka, Enugu State, serving delicious Unique cuisine cooked by the best chefs. Quality

Our strong culture and values are part of our core purpose and what makes us a successful company.


Customer Service

We pride ourselves on delivering great products and services to our customers every day. The essence of our business is to satisfy our customers. We made customer feedback available for our consumers to inform us of their suggestions and opinions in order to improve our customer service. Our resturant is very detailed with name tags in all sections and well labelled prices on resturant shelves, making it easier for our valued customers to navigate the resturant and identify their desired products. We have uniformed staff on all sections of the resturant ready to deliver the best possible service for our customers.

Varieties

We are a one-stop resturant that sells virtually everything you need to eat, also one of the leading resturant in Nigeria.
